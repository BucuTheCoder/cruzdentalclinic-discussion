A Web-Based Appointment and Patient Record Management System for Cruz Dental Clinic

Capstone Project - Cruz Dental Clinic

About The Project

The project, titled "Web-Based Appointment and Patient Record Management System for Cruz Dental Clinic," is a capstone initiative aimed at modernizing the clinic's operations. This system addresses the inefficiencies of their current appointment booking process, where patients have to call the receptionist to schedule visits. By introducing an online booking system, the project streamlines appointment management, allowing patients to easily schedule appointments with their preferred dentist. Additionally, the project aims to reduce the clinic's reliance on paper records by digitizing patient histories and procedures, ensuring that all records are securely stored and easily accessible. This web-based solution not only improves the clinic's operational efficiency but also enhances the overall patient experience, making it easier for the clinic to manage appointments and maintain accurate records.

My Role

In this project, I served as a full stack developer, contributing to both the user interface and backend development. I utilized PHP and SQL to build and manage the backend, ensuring that the web application effectively handles and manipulates patient data. My primary focus was on developing the appointment feature, which allows patients to book appointments online seamlessly. This feature is central to the project's goal of improving the clinic's patient handling process, replacing the traditional phone-based booking system with a more efficient and user-friendly web-based solution.

Features of the Project

  • Patient Appointment Booking: Patients can easily book their appointments online through the web app.
  • Receptionist Appointment Management: Receptionists can book appointments on behalf of patients, ensuring flexibility in scheduling.
  • Dentist File Management: Dentists can manage patient files, including inputting treatment details for specific teeth.
  • Treatment Record Keeping: Enables detailed record-keeping of treatments performed on each patient, linked to specific teeth.
  • User Role Management: Different user roles (patient, receptionist, dentist) with specific permissions tailored to their needs.
  • Responsive Design: Frontend is built with Bootstrap to ensure the application is accessible on various devices.
  • Secure Data Handling: Ensures that all patient records and appointments are securely stored and accessible only to authorized users.
